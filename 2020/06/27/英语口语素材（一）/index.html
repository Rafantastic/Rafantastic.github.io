发表于 2020-06-27 分类于 English ， Oral
英语失宠太久了，要哄哄哈哈哈哈

via: 《实用外教口语课》（BV17b411A7zt）

Lesson 1 How to start a conversation

Conversational English

打招呼

  • Hello/Hi/How’s it going?/How are you doing?/Hey, what’s up?
  • How is/How’s/How are… …怎么样
    1. How is everything?
    2. How’s life?
    3. How’s your family?
    4. How’s your job?
    5. How are your parents?
    6. How are your family?
  • I’m/They’re doing great./Pretty good

花式搭讪

  • Wow, I really love your …(Skirt/Coat/Shoes/Hairstyle)
  • Wow, that’s a really cool …(Camera/Phone/Jacket)
  • Excuse me, I just heard you speaking Chinese, you’re pretty good!
  • Excuse me, is this seat taken / is anyone sitting here?
  • This place is pretty cool. Do you come here often?
  • Do you need a hand?

自我介绍

  • Hi, nice to meet you. I’m Chris.
  • I’m Chris. / My name is Chris.
  • Allow me to introduce myself. I’m Chris.
  • This is my business card.
  • I’ve heard a lot of things about you.(久仰大名)
  • Chris has told me all about you.(…经常跟我提起你)

如何回应对方的自我介绍

  • I’d like you to meet a friend of mine.
  • Let me introduce you to my friend Li Ning.
  • This is Li Ning. Li Ning, Chris.

如何婉转的问对方的名字

  • Excuse me, but I don’t think I know your name.
  • I’m so sorry. Could you tell me your name again?
  • I’m sorry, but I don’t believe I know your name.
  • (It’s) nice to meet you. / Pleased to meet you. / It’s a pleasure to meet you.

"Nice to meet you"之后说什么

  • So, have you lived\worked here long?
  • Do you live around here?
  • Do you come here often?

如何谈论职业

  • So, what do you do, if you don’t mind me asking?
  • So what exaclty is it that you do?
  • So what do you do for a living?
  • Can I ask you what you do?

在中国与老外谈什么

  • So, how did you end up in China?
  • Are you used to the life in China?
  • What places have you visited in China?
  • What is your favorite city in China?
  • What do you think of China/Chinese food/Chinese girls/Beijing?

介绍自己的公司/业务

  • I’m a software engineer.
  • I’m in …(IT/Finance)
  • I work for a/an … company.(State-owned company国企/Chinese company/Foreign company外企/Insurance company)
