英语口语素材（一）

发表于 2020-06-27 更新于 2020-07-02 分类于 English ， Oral
英语失宠太久了，要哄哄哈哈哈哈

via: 《实用外教口语课》（BV17b411A7zt）

Lesson 1 How to start a conversation

Conversational English

打招呼

  • Hello/Hi/How’s it going?/How are you doing?/Hey, what’s up?
  • How is/How’s/How are… …怎么样
    1. How is everything?
    2. How’s life?
    3. How’s your family?
    4. How’s your job?
    5. How are your parents?
    6. How are your family?
  • I’m/They’re doing great./Pretty good

花式搭讪

  • Wow, I really love your …(Skirt/Coat/Shoes/Hairstyle)
  • Wow, that’s a really cool …(Camera/Phone/Jacket)
  • Excuse me, I just heard you speaking Chinese, you’re pretty good!
  • Excuse me, is this seat taken / is anyone sitting here?
  • This place is pretty cool. Do you come here often?
  • Do you need a hand?

自我介绍

  • Hi, nice to meet you. I’m Chris.
  • I’m Chris. / My name is Chris.
  • Allow me to introduce myself. I’m Chris.
  • This is my business card.
  • I’ve heard a lot of things about you.(久仰大名)
  • Chris has told me all about you.(…经常跟我提起你)

如何回应对方的自我介绍

  • I’d like you to meet a friend of mine.
  • Let me introduce you to my friend Li Ning.
  • This is Li Ning. Li Ning, Chris.

如何婉转的问对方的名字

  • Excuse me, but I don’t think I know your name.
  • I’m so sorry. Could you tell me your name again?
  • I’m sorry, but I don’t believe I know your name.
  • (It’s) nice to meet you. / Pleased to meet you. / It’s a pleasure to meet you.

"Nice to meet you"之后说什么

  • So, have you lived\worked here long?
  • Do you live around here?
  • Do you come here often?

如何谈论职业

  • So, what do you do, if you don’t mind me asking?
  • So what exaclty is it that you do?
  • So what do you do for a living?
  • Can I ask you what you do?

在中国与老外谈什么

  • So, how did you end up in China?
  • Are you used to the life in China?
  • What places have you visited in China?
  • What is your favorite city in China?
  • What do you think of China/Chinese food/Chinese girls/Beijing?

介绍自己的公司/业务

  • I’m a software engineer.
  • I’m in …(IT/Finance)
  • I work for a/an … company.(State-owned company国企/Chinese company/Foreign company外企/Insurance company)

Lesson 2 Daily conversation topics

问及家庭

  • Do you have any brothers and sisters?
  • I’ve got an older/younger brother/sister
  • Do you have a big family?
  • Where are your family from?
  • Are they all back in the US? 他们都在美国吗？
  • Are you married?
  • Do you have kids yet?
  • Do you have a family yet? 你有孩子吗？(Family=配偶+子女)
  • Where do your parents do?

兴趣爱好

  • So, what do you like to do in your spare time?
    • outside work 工作之余
    • to relax 放松的时候
  • What are your hobbies?
  • What are you interested in?

聊聊自己的兴趣爱好

  • I like dancing, travelling and taking photos.
  • I love cooking and reading.
  • I’m crazy about playing basketball.
  • I’m really interested in history.
  • I’m passionate about

聊聊平时做事的频率

  • Do you often play football?
  • Do you get to play football much?
  • Do you have much opportunity to play football?
  • No, not very often.
  • I play football(every week)
  • once/twice/three times a week
  • from time to time 偶尔
  • when I get the time 有时间的时候

聊体育运动

  • So, what sports do you like to (play/watch)?

聊支持的球队

  • What team do you support?
  • Do you have a favourite team?
  • I support Liverpool.
  • I don’t really support a particular team.

聊健身

  • So what do you do to keep fit?
  • Do you like going to the gym?
  • Do you work out?
  • I like to go for a run in the mornings.

赞美别人

  • You look gorgeous in that dress!
  • You’re looking great today!
  • You look very healthy/buff.
  • Have you lost weight?

问对方的偏好

  • What kind of (movies/sport/music/TV shows/places) do you like?
  • I like watching (action movies/comedies/horror movies/science-fiction movies).
  • I like (pop/rock/classical/jazz) music.
  • Who is your favourite singer?
  • Are you a fan of Jay Chou?
  • Do you often go to concerts/gigs?
  • Do you play a musical instrument?
  • What TV series are you watching at the moment?
  • I’m binge-watching House of Cards. 刷/追剧

聊旅游

  • Where are you going for your holidays this year?
  • Where did you go for your vacation?
  • I went to the Maldives.
  • Did you have a good trip?
  • Yes I really enjoyed myself.
  • Where did you go in America?
  • Where would you most like to visit?
  • Have you been to France before?
  • Have you ever been to France?
  • What places have you been to in China?

Lesson 3 How to end a conversation skilfully

当时处于哈里王子大婚的时期哈哈哈

  • Did you see the wedding?
  • Congratulations!
  • Congratulations on your wedding!

告别

  • See you (later)!
  • See you this weekend!
  • See you around!
  • Have a good (day/night/evening)!
  • Have a good one. 祝你度过愉快的一天
  • Hope to see you again soon.

如何巧妙地中断对话

  • Excuse me for a minute, I’ll be right back.
  • Well, I’ve got to go/run.
  • Well, I should be going.
  • Hey, I should go, or I’ll be late for …/work/school/my meeting/my train/my flight
  • Well, I won’t bother you any more.
  • Ok, I won’t take up any more of your time.
  • I should go say hi to my friend.
  • I should get back to my work.
  • I should let you get back to your work.

如何礼貌地结束对话

  • I really enjoyed your chat.
  • It was great to talk to you today.
  • It was really nice meeting you.
  • It was really nice to talk to you.
  • I had a great time talking with you, but I need to get going or I’ll be late.

互留联系方式

  • Let me give you my email address.
  • Feel free to call me if you want to hang out.Here, I’ll give you my number.
  • Do you have WeChat?
  • Can I add you on WeChat?
  • Shall I scan you, or do you want to scan me?
  • Can I get your phone number?
  • Stay in touch / keep in touch!

安排下次再聚

  • Let’s go for a coffee sometime.
  • I’d love to show you some cool places.
  • I’d love to hang out sometime, I’ll call you, ok?
  • Are you free any time next week?
  • When are you free?
  • Are you free on Saturday at 3pm?

帮我带去问候

  • Say hello to (your parents / your wife / your husband / John) for me.
  • Send my regards to (your family).
  • Give (your family) my love.
  • Keep in touch.
  • Look after yourself.
  • Take care!

Lesson4 Expressing thanks, sorry and opinions

表达不同程度的感谢

  • Thanks
  • Thank you
  • Thanks a lot
  • Thanks a bunch
  • Thanks a million
  • Thank you anyway/all the same.

其它的谢谢

  • I just wanted to call and say thank you again.
  • I definitely owe you a favour.
  • I owe you one.
  • Thank you for (your help / coming / the gift / the flowers / hosting me)

万分感激

  • I really appreciate everything you’ve done.
  • I don’t know how to thank you.
  • I don’t know what to say.
  • I really can’t find the words to express my thanks.

不客气

  • You’re (very) welcome.
  • That’s quite alright.
  • Don’t mention it.
  • My pleasure.
  • No need to thank me.

道歉

  • Sorry.
  • I’m sorry.
  • Sorry I’m late.
  • I’m sorry to trouble you.
  • I’m sorry to (interrupt).
  • I’m really sorry for (interrupting you / the mistake / what I did / arriving late).
  • I want to apologize for (interrupting) you.
  • I apologise.

真诚地请求原谅

  • Can you forgive me?
  • Please forgive me.
  • I didn’t mean it.
  • It wasn’t intentional.
  • I didn’t do it on purpose.

接受道歉

  • Never mind.
  • That’s ok. Don’t worry about it.
  • It’s not a big deal.
  • That’s alright.
  • No worries.
  • I accept your apology. It really wasn’t a very big deal.

表达祝贺

  • Congratulations!
  • Congratulations (on your ${18}^{th}$ birthday / on the birth of your son/daughter / on your wedding).

表达祝福

  • I wish you (happiness and good health / success / in your exams/career)!
  • I hope you are successful!
  • Have a good (trip / time / weekend / day)!
  • Good luck!
  • Best of luck!
  • All the best!
  • Have fun!

表示欢迎

  • Welcome to (China / our company)!
  • Come back again soon

询问意见

  • What do you think of (this reataurant).
  • What’s your opinion of…?
  • How do you feel about …?
  • I like it.
  • I don’t really like it.

表示赞同

  • What do you think?
  • Yes, I agree.
  • I completely agree.
  • I couldn’t agree more.
  • You’re absolutely right.
  • That’s a good point.
  • Yes, of course!

表示反对

  • I don’t agree / I disagree.
  • I don’t think that’s right.
  • I have a different opinion.
  • I’m afraid I have to disagree.

Lesson 5 At the airport + changing money

关键词汇

  • Airport / Tichket / Passport / Visa
  • Terminal 1 1号航站楼
  • Check in 办理值机手续
  • Check in desk 值机柜台
  • Tichket
  • Boarding pass 登机牌
  • Boarding gate 登机口
  • Customs 海关/安检

办理值机时

  • Excuse me, do you know where I can check in?
  • Where are you travelling to today?
  • Could I see your passport, please.
  • Here is my passport / visa.
  • Do you have ang luggage to check in / carry on baggage?(托运/随身)
  • Can I take this bag on board?
  • Luggage / Baggage / Bag / Suitcase

如何要求一个靠窗的座位

  • Do you have any window(aisle) seats?(靠窗/靠通道)
  • Can we sit together?
  • Do you have seats together?

登机口在哪里

  • What time do I need to board?
  • Which boarding gate do I need to go to?
  • Where is the security check / customs?
  • Where is the duty free shop?

上飞机

  • Excuse me, where is my seat?
  • What time does this flight arrive in New York?
  • How long before we arrive?
  • Could you give me (a blanket / a pillow / a pair of slippers / some earphones / a cup of water) please?

取行李

  • Where can I pick up my luggage?
  • Luggage claim area / baggage claim 行李提取处
  • Excuse me, my bag is missing.
  • Here is my luggage tag.(行李签)

换汇

  • Excuse me, can I change money here?
  • What is the exchange rate?
  • I would like to change 1000 (Chinese yuan) into (US dollars). (Euros / Pounds / Yen / Hong Kong dollars / Australian dollars / Singapore dollars)
  • Is there a service charge / any commission?

退税

  • Where can I get a VAT(增值税) refund / tax refund?
  • Can I get a tax refund on these goods?
  • Here is the receipt / my passport.
  • Could I get the refund in cash?

入关

  • How long will you be staying in the US?
  • Where will you be staying?
  • What is the purpose of your trip?
  • It’s a business trip / vacation.
  • I’m here to see friends / study?
  • Do you have anything to declare(报关)?
